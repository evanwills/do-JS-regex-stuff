Match unfinished payment IDs to confirmed payments.

There are many reasons why payments in the form_build system end up as being incomplete. Most of these are due people not actually getting to the point where they hand over money. But some are caused by them not reaching the payment response page (where the payment system reconciles their payment with the things they've paid for.

If a user has actually made a payment and ACU has recived that money then there'll be a record in the Finance system. In these cases you can follow these steps and move payments from "Unfinished" to "Payments".

  1. Find out the acronym for your payment form
  2. Contact finance and request a list of all the payments made to that acronym
    You only need the Transaction Reference ID.
  3. Once you have the list from finance, copy all the Transaction Reference IDs
  4. Paste the copied Transaction Reference IDs into the Transaction Reference IDs (from finance) field
  5. Go to the form build "Unfinished" payments interface and copy all the unfinished payments listed there
  6. Paste the copied listing of unfinished payments into the Copied "Unfinished" payments listing field
  7. Then click the "MODIFY INPUT" button on the bottom left

    NOTE: processing the data is very quick. It's possible that you won't notice anything change.
  8. What remains in the Copied "Unfinished" payments listing field are the "Unfinished" payments that have matching Transaction Reference IDs and thus, can be considered paid.
  9. Copy the contnts of Copied "Unfinished" payments listing field to somewhere else (e.g. Excel or Notepad)
  10. Now for the boring and tedious part:
  11. Select and copy the first ID in the list.
  12. Go back to Form build admin, to the "Unfinished" payments interface ad search the page for the ID you just copied.
  13. When you find the ID, click the "Complete" button to move the payment into the "Payments" area. The user will then show up in the bookings listings and in the export.
  14. Repeat steps 11, 12 & 13 for all the confirmed payments.
  15. If you wish to provide users with a receipt, go to the "Payments" interface.
  16. (in the list you copied into the somewhere else (e.g. Excel or Notepad) Select and copy the first ID in the list.
  17. Back in the "Payments" interface, search for the ID.
  18. Click on the "More" link
  19. Select & copy the email address into a new email
  20. Close the "More" modal (popup)
  21. Click on the PDF button and save the PDF to your local computer
  22. Attach the PDF to the email and add what niceties you think are appropriate then send the email.
  23. Repeat steps 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21 & 22 for all the confirmed payments.
